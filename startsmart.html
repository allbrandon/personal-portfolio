StartSmart - Mobile App

UI/UX

2019 UNSW Hero Progam: Top 10

Theme: Solving Student Problems at UNSW

StartSmart is a mobile app that takes first-years through a gamified experience, inspiring them to expand their professional network and develop their interpersonal skills. StartSmart ignites the professionals within first-years, nurturing ‘In-Demand Graduates’ who are prepared for the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century.

Skill Select

Users pick professional or soft skills that they are interested in developing.

User Profile

Students create a customisable avatar and can see how much experience they have gained for their skills, which can be gained by going to events.

Main Screen

This is the map where students can view events that are happening on campus. Icons are displayed to tell users what skills they will gain experience for by going to the event. They also gain Spark points which are currency, further used to customise their avatar.

Event Page

Upon clicking on an event, details about the event are displayed.

This includes their friends that are going, the time, date and location and how much experience/Spark points they will gain
