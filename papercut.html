PaperCut - Digital Receipts Web App

UI/UX & HTML/CSS

2019 CSESoc Hackathon Winner

Theme: UN Sustainability Goal - Sustainable Production and Consumption

Aiming to eliminate the wastage and cumbersome nature of paper receipts, this web-app was designed as a centralised platform where customers can store digital receipts, both from online and offline. Customers use NFC at checkout to get their digital receipt.

Main Screen

This is the main screen where users can view a list of businesses that they have stored a receipt for.

Receipt Menu

Once users have clicked on a business, they can view all the receipts that they have added.

Receipt Display

Upon clicking a certain receipt to view, the full receipt is displayed showing all details that would be found on a physical receipt.
